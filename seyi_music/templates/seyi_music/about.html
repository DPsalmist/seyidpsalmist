About Me

Bakare O. Anthony

Bakare Oluwaseyi Anthony populary known as Seyi Dpsalmist is a native of Okerimi-Oro, Irepo Local Government, kwara State, Nigeria. He was born and brought up in Lagos.

He started his music career in 2006 in afellowship under Mountain of Fire and Miracles (MFM) and teh Dominion Apostolic Faith Church. He later joined the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) in the year 2012 where he has been till date. He is a Deacon and happily married to Victoria Ola Afolabi and blessed with children.

Seyi Dpsalmist has ministered far and wide within and outside Nigeria in various church programs and events. He released his debut album titled Ko S'Oba Bi Ire on the 8th of Deceber, 2019.
